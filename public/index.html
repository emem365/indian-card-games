How to Play Chaugadi

  • Teams: 4 players, 2 teams (Opposite players are partners).
  • Objective: Win rounds (tricks) by playing the highest card.
  • Setup: 52 cards. Ace is high.
  • Bidding: Deal 5 cards. Bid how many tricks your team can win (Min 7). Highest bidder chooses Trump.
  • Play: Highest bidder leads. Must follow suit. Trump wins if you can't follow suit and play a Trump. Highest card wins the trick.
  • Winning: Win at least the number of tricks you bid to score points.
